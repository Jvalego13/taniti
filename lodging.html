Lodging

Taniti offers a wide variety of lodging options, ranging from budget-friendly hostels to luxurious, four-star resorts. There are many small, family-owned hotels, as well as a growing number of bed-and-breakfast establishments. All types of lodging are strictly regulated and regularly inspected by the Tanitian government.
